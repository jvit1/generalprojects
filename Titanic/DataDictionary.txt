PassengerId- Unique identifier for each passenger.
Pclass- Status level of ticket
Sex- Sex of passenger
Age- Age of passenger
SibSp- Number of siblings or spouses onboard
Parch- Number of parents or children onboard
Ticket- Ticket identificaiton number/string
Fare- Cost of ticket
Cabin- Location onboard
Embarked- Location of where passenger left from
